# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying accountable for impact.
- Keep criticism focused on code, design, and project outcomes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruption of project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
